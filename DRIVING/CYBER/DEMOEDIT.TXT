
							DEMONSTRATION RELEASE

'Construction' is not available in this version.  In the commercial release
a complete level constructor is included.  This level constructor is the same
one we used to create all of our levels.  It's features include:

	-   WYSIWYG 3D editor.
	-   Ability to get on a CyberByke and try new things right inside the
		editor.
	-   Ability to cut and paste between different levels.
	-   Easy trading of newly created levels with others. (Simply trade .LVL
		files and they automatically appear on the world map where you
		placed them.)
	-   Customize colors, background bitmaps, music, gravity level, lighting,
		and much more.
	-   Easy to learn and use.

The CD-ROM version of the game also includes high resolution artwork and
animations.  The CD-ROM disc is not required in the drive for gameplay.
