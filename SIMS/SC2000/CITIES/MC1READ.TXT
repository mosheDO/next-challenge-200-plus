This is the first city that I did for SimCity 2000. It was started in the year 1900 on the easy setting. The city grew to about 120,000 and then I started redesigning areas of it for Arcos.  The interesting thing that happens with arcos is that they are non-zone and as i started redesigning areas the demand for various zones dropped and caused numerous problems. You will notice that there is one residential, one commercial and one industrial zone left. If you remove any one of these the city will go into chaos. The roads that are left are not needed and could be removed. This is also true about the airport and seaport. The city makes about 30,000 dollars a at 2% tax rate. The mayor's rating stays between 78% and 90% but it has shot up to 96%. The major problems of the city are pollution, education, crime, and healthcare. Surprisingly traffic is not a problem. I am currently working on a city that hopefully reach 10,000,000 sims, I don't think that you can get a city to much above that. This may not be the best looking city, but I hope it gives you some ideas about very big cities.
