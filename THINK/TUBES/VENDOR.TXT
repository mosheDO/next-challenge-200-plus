SHORT DESCRIPTION:

Addictive action packed puzzle game.  Match similar coloured atoms
to make them disappear.  Requires: VGA,286+  Supports: Adlib, SB, GM


MEDIUM DESCRIPTION:

Addictive action packed puzzle game.  Match at least 3 similar
coloured atoms to make them disappear in a display of fantastic
animation.  Impressive digital sound on the PC Speaker & Sound
Blaster with an original score for the Adlib & General Midi.
Requires: VGA,286+


LONG DESCRIPTION:

Addictive action packed puzzle game.  You're Professor Lanny B.
Brilliant's assistant and your goal is to create new molecule
chains by matching at least 3 similiar coloured atoms.  Watch
as the molecule chains disappear in a fantastic display of
animation.  Impressive digital sound on the PC Speaker & Sound
Blaster with an original score for the Adlib & General Midi.
Requires: VGA,286+


REQUIREMENTS:

- At least a 286 processor.  A 386, 486, or Pentium is fine.
- 420K of free conventional memory is the minimum.  Sound will require an
  additional 65K and Music will require 25K.  Total memory requirements
  are 510K.
- A VGA card with at least 256K.  Super VGA cards are fine.

- General Midi
