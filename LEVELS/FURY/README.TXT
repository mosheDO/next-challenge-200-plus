Using either the joystick or the keyboard (arrows and Space Bar), find the
exit of each of the 3 levels, chosen among the 90 of this Arcade game. Watch
out! You have a limited amount of time to find the exit.

Your TINY can: 
 - swim above and under water (blue color)
 - throw balls of fire (yellow color)
 - use a rope to move about and grab objects (green color)
 - eat some elements of the scenery (red color).
