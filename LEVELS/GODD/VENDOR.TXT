================
Vinyl Goddess From Mars can only be described as a SEXY platform game.  While
en-route to the esteemed intergalactic B movie festival, the Vinyl Goddess' 
space ship is engulfed in a sea of rock and debris.  Veering off coarse, she 
is forced to crash land on a strange and forbidding planet.  The force of the
impact scatters her precious storage capsules about this strange planet.  It's
up to you to guide this beautiful goddess through dangerous and exotic 
locations while collecting her lost storage capsules.  Can the Vinyl Goddess 
reach the festival in time?  GET READY!  Here comes this new and exciting 
release from UNION LOGIC SOFTWARE PUBLISHING that will blow your mind at 70 
frames per second.  Vinyl Goddess From Mars features beautifully detailed 
graphics in 256 wonderful colors, a heart pounding soundtrack and full 
digital sound f/x for SoundBlaster(tm) and compatible sound cards.

MEDIUM LENGTH DESCRIPTION
=========================
Vinyl Goddess From Mars is a SEXY platform game.  The Vinyl Goddess has crash
landed on a strange planet.  Help Vinyl recover enough storage capsules and 
find her way off the planet in order to reach the intergalactic B movie 
festival on time.  This game features 70 frames per second smooth scrolling, 
a heart pounding soundtrack and full digital sound f/x for SoundBlaster(tm) 
and compatible sound cards.

Vinyl Goddess is a SEXY platform game with smooth scrolling and SoundBlaster
