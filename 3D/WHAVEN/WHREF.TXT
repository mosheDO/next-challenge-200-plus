WITCHAVEN

At the DOS prompt type 'whaven'.  Select your audio configuration. 
When that's done, set your game preferences (mode, inputs, etc...).

There is no status bar in SVGA.  Select 'Help' for more complete 
information.

SPELLS:

F1- Scare
F2- Night Vision
F3- Freeze
F4- Magic Arrows
F5- Open Locked Doors
F6- Fly
Press 'alt' to cast a particular spell.
Press 'end' to cancel the fly spell.

CONTROLS:

CTRL:        Attack
PAGE UP:     Look up
PAGE DOWN:   Look Down
HOME:        Center View
A or INS:    Jump up
Z or DEL:    Duck 
UP ARROW:    Move Forward
DOWN ARROW:  Move Backward
LEFT ARROW:  Turn Left
RIGHT ARROW: Turn Right
TAB:         Map
+:           Zoom In
-:           Zoom Out
SPACE BAR:   Open Doors

POTIONS:

From left to right:
HEALTH
STRENGTH
ARMOR
FIRE WALK
INVISIBLE

SVGA- Scroll through potions.

WEAPONS:

1: Fists
2: Dagger
3: Morning Star
4: Bow and Arrow
5: Magic Sword
