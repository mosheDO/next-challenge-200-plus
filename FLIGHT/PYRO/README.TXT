Pyrotechnica 
 
The year is 2112. Inter planetary research shows information capsules 
buried deep within the hearts of ancient stars. Knowledge that long dead 
civilisations left behind light years ago.  And data retrieval is big business. 
The Terran Hardlight Corporation pay Runners billions of Creds to 
excavate star cores . There is just one problem though. Adherents. Hostile 
alien cyborgs that were programmed to defend this knowledge. At all 
costs. Without mercy......  
 
Now, a team of Wingmen Runners are pinned down in the Halls of Fire, 
near the centre of the massive Red Star. The Corporation needs them out 
of there. And rescue rights go to the highest bidder... 
 
As a Net Runner you specialise in this type of operation. In this dazzling 
search and rescue shoot em up, pilot your space corvette through spiralling 
tunnels and chambers. Rescue your Wingmen and take out enemy 
platforms  before launching screeching missile runs against Adherent 
installations. With  explosive 3D Gouraud shaded graphics and 
nauseatingly  fast, arcade action gameplay, Pyrotechnica is a riotous 
Molotov cocktail of a game that will set the PC world ablaze. 
 
Features Include 
 
True 3-D gaming environment with fast Gouraud shaded, depth cued 
graphics which enhance the feeling of movement 
 
 Advanced light sourcing creates a dazzling firework effect during combat 
 Alternate 2 player mode 
 
Dozens of power-ups, craft enhancements and a feast of weaponry. 
Rescued wingmen join in the dogfight!  
 
 Thrashing techno soundtrack 
 
Pyrotechnica 
 
Available on PC 13th April 1995.
