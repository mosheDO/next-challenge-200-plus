The Goblins trilogy have captured the hearts and minds of PC 
games players all over the world. As a tribute to their success, 
we have grouped them together in this massive demo of their games. 
Each Goblin is able to perform one task (such as picking something 
up) and together, these funny creatures must work together as a team 
to solve puzzles so twisted and humorous, that they would look out of 
place on It's a Knockout. Cute graphics and sound only enhance the 
playability of these games, which remain many PC gamers firm 
favourites to this day.
